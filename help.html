If you've forgotten your password, you can easily reset it by following these steps:

  1. Go to the login page and click on "Forgot Password?"
  2. Enter your registered email address
  3. Check your email for a password reset link
  4. Click the link and create a new password
  5. Log in with your new password

Note: The reset link expires after 24 hours. If you don't receive the email, check your spam folder.

Yes! You can download articles for offline reading with a Premium subscription:

  • Open any article you want to save
  • Tap the download icon (arrow pointing down) at the top of the article
  • The article will be saved to your device
  • Access downloaded articles from the "Downloaded" section in the sidebar

Downloaded articles are available for 30 days and will automatically refresh when you're online.

We're sorry to see you go! To cancel your subscription:

  1. Go to Settings from the sidebar
  2. Click on "Subscription & Billing"
  3. Select "Manage Subscription"
  4. Click "Cancel Subscription" at the bottom
  5. Confirm your cancellation

Your subscription will remain active until the end of your current billing period. You can reactivate anytime.

You have full control over which emails you receive from us:

  • Navigate to Settings > Notifications
  • Toggle email preferences for different categories:
    • Daily digest of trending articles
    • New articles from followed authors
    • Comments on your saved articles
    • Weekly newsletter
  • Save your preferences

Changes take effect immediately. You'll always receive important account-related emails.

Having trouble logging in? Try these solutions:

  1. Clear your browser cache and cookies - Old data can cause login issues
  2. Check your internet connection - Ensure you have a stable connection
  3. Verify your email and password - Make sure there are no typos
  4. Try a different browser - Sometimes browser extensions can interfere
  5. Reset your password - Use the "Forgot Password?" link if needed
  6. Disable VPN temporarily - Some VPNs may block access

If none of these work, contact our support team for immediate assistance.
