Offline Reading

Premium Feature

Offline reading is available exclusively for Premium subscribers. Download articles and read them anytime, anywhere without an internet connection.

How to download articles for offline reading
  1. Open the article you want to download
  2. Tap the download icon (↓) at the top of the article
  3. Wait for the download to complete (indicated by a checkmark)
  4. Access your downloaded articles from the "Downloaded" section in the sidebar

Tip: You can download multiple articles at once by selecting them from your reading list.

How long do downloaded articles stay available?

Downloaded articles remain available for 30 days from the download date. After that:

  • Articles will automatically refresh when you're online
  • You can manually refresh by tapping the refresh icon
  • Expired downloads are marked with a warning icon
  • Simply re-download to access them offline again
How much storage do downloads use?

Storage usage varies by article:

  • Text-only articles: ~50-200 KB
  • Articles with images: ~500 KB - 2 MB
  • Articles with videos: ~5-20 MB

You can check your total download storage in Settings > Storage Management.

Can I download articles on multiple devices?

Yes! With a Premium subscription, you can:

  • Download articles on up to 5 devices simultaneously
  • Sync your downloads across all devices
  • Manage downloads independently on each device
  • Reading progress syncs automatically when online
