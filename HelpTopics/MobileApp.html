Troubleshooting

App crashes or freezes

Try these solutions:

  1. Force close the app and reopen it
  2. Check for app updates in the App Store/Play Store
  3. Restart your device
  4. Clear the app cache (Settings > Apps > Bace > Clear Cache)
  5. Uninstall and reinstall the app (your data will be preserved)
Sync issues between devices

If your data isn't syncing:

  • Ensure you're logged into the same account on all devices
  • Check your internet connection
  • Pull down to refresh on the home screen
  • Sign out and sign back in
Can't download articles

Check the following:

  • • Verify you have an active Premium subscription
  • • Ensure you have enough storage space on your device
  • • Check your internet connection
  • • Try downloading a different article to test
  • • Clear app cache and try again
Notifications not working

To fix notification issues:

  1. Check notification settings in the Bace app
  2. Verify notifications are enabled in your device settings:
    • iOS: Settings > Notifications > Bace
    • Android: Settings > Apps > Bace > Notifications
  3. Ensure "Do Not Disturb" mode is off
  4. Check battery optimization settings (Android)
How to update the app

iOS:

  1. Open the App Store
  2. Tap your profile icon in the top right
  3. Scroll to find Bace
  4. Tap "Update" if available

Android:

  1. Open Google Play Store
  2. Tap your profile icon
  3. Select "Manage apps & device"
  4. Find Bace and tap "Update"
