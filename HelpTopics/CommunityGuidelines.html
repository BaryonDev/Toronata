Community Guidelines

Our rules for creating a respectful, safe, and welcoming community for everyone.

Our Core Values

Bace is built on respect, inclusivity, and meaningful conversation. We expect all community members to uphold these values.

Be Respectful

Treat others with kindness and consideration

Be Inclusive

Welcome diverse perspectives and backgrounds

Be Constructive

Contribute meaningfully to discussions

What We Encourage

Thoughtful Discussions

Share your insights, ask questions, and engage in meaningful conversations. We love when our community learns from each other.

Diverse Perspectives

We welcome different viewpoints and encourage respectful debate. Disagreement is fine, but always remain civil.

Constructive Feedback

When critiquing articles or comments, focus on the content, not the person. Offer suggestions and alternative viewpoints.

Fact-Based Contributions

Support your arguments with credible sources. If you're sharing information, make sure it's accurate and verifiable.

Reporting Issues

Help us maintain a safe community by reporting content that violates our guidelines. Your reports are confidential.

What We Don't Allow

Hate Speech & Discrimination

Content that promotes hatred, violence, or discrimination based on race, ethnicity, religion, gender, sexual orientation, disability, or any other protected characteristic is strictly prohibited.

Harassment & Bullying

Personal attacks, threats, doxxing, or any form of harassment will not be tolerated. This includes targeted harassment of authors or other community members.

Misinformation & Fake News

Deliberately spreading false information, conspiracy theories, or misleading content is not allowed. Always verify facts before sharing.

Spam & Self-Promotion

Excessive self-promotion, spam, or irrelevant links are not permitted. Share content that adds value to the community.

Explicit or Inappropriate Content

Sexually explicit material, graphic violence, or other inappropriate content is prohibited. Keep discussions professional and appropriate for all ages.

Impersonation

Pretending to be someone else, including authors, public figures, or Bace staff, is not allowed. Be authentic.

Illegal Activities

Content promoting or facilitating illegal activities, including piracy, fraud, or violence, will result in immediate account suspension.

Enforcement & Consequences

How we enforce guidelines

Our moderation team reviews reported content and takes appropriate action based on the severity of the violation:

1

Warning

First-time minor violations receive a warning and content removal.

2

Temporary Suspension

Repeated violations or moderate offenses result in 7-30 day suspensions.

3

Permanent Ban

Severe violations or repeated offenses lead to permanent account termination.

Appeal process

If you believe your content was removed or your account was suspended in error, you can appeal:

  1. Email appeals@bace.com with your account details
  2. Explain why you believe the decision was incorrect
  3. Our team will review within 3-5 business days
  4. You'll receive a final decision via email

How to Report Violations

If you see content that violates our guidelines, please report it immediately. Your reports help keep our community safe.

Report a comment

  1. Click the three dots (⋯) on the comment
  2. Select "Report"
  3. Choose the violation type
  4. Add additional context (optional)
  5. Submit the report

Report an article

  1. Click the flag icon at the top of the article
  2. Select the reason for reporting
  3. Provide details about the issue
  4. Submit for review

Note: All reports are confidential. The reported user will not know who submitted the report. False or malicious reports may result in action against your account.

Help us build a better community

Together, we can create a space where everyone feels welcome, respected, and heard. Thank you for being part of Bace.
