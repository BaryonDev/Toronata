Creating Your Account

Sign up in 3 easy steps

1

Visit the sign-up page

Click "Sign Up" in the top right corner of any page or go directly to bace.com/signup

2

Enter your information

Provide your email address, create a password, and choose a display name

Or sign up quickly with Google, Apple, or Facebook

3

Verify your email

Check your inbox for a verification email and click the link to activate your account

What information do I need to sign up?

To create a Bace account, you'll need:

  • A valid email address
  • A secure password (at least 8 characters)
  • A display name (can be changed later)

Optional: Profile picture, bio, and interests can be added after signup.

Can I sign up with social media?

Yes! You can sign up using:

  • Google account
  • Apple ID
  • Facebook account

This allows for quick signup without creating a new password. Your social media credentials are never stored by Bace.

I didn't receive the verification email

If you haven't received your verification email:

  1. Check your spam or junk folder
  2. Make sure you entered the correct email address
  3. Wait a few minutes - emails can sometimes be delayed
  4. Click "Resend verification email" on the login page
  5. Contact support if you still don't receive it after 30 minutes

How to edit your profile

  1. Click on your profile picture in the sidebar
  2. Select "Edit Profile"
  3. Make your desired changes
  4. Click "Save Changes" at the bottom
How do I change my email address?
  1. Go to Settings > Account
  2. Click "Change Email"
  3. Enter your new email address
  4. Verify with your current password
  5. Check both old and new email for confirmation links
  6. Click the link in your new email to complete the change

Note: You'll need to verify the new email before it becomes active.

Can I change my username?

Yes, you can change your display name anytime:

  1. Go to your profile settings
  2. Click on your current display name
  3. Enter your new name
  4. Save changes

Your username can be changed once every 30 days.

How do I delete my account?

We're sorry to see you go. To delete your account:

  1. Go to Settings > Account > Delete Account
  2. Read the information about what will be deleted
  3. Enter your password to confirm
  4. Click "Permanently Delete Account"

Warning: This action is permanent and cannot be undone. All your data, saved articles, and reading history will be deleted.
