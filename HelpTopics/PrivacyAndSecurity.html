Account Security

Enable Two-Factor Authentication (2FA)

Add an extra layer of security to your account with 2FA:

  1. Go to Settings > Security
  2. Click "Enable Two-Factor Authentication"
  3. Choose your preferred method (SMS or Authenticator App)
  4. Follow the setup instructions
  5. Save your backup codes in a safe place

Important: Keep your backup codes safe. You'll need them if you lose access to your 2FA device.

Create a strong password

Follow these best practices for a secure password:

  • Use at least 12 characters
  • Mix uppercase, lowercase, numbers, and symbols
  • Avoid common words or personal information
  • Use a unique password for Bace (don't reuse)
  • Consider using a password manager
Manage active sessions

View and manage all devices where you're logged in:

  1. Navigate to Settings > Security > Active Sessions
  2. Review the list of devices and locations
  3. Click "Sign Out" next to any suspicious sessions
  4. Use "Sign Out All Devices" if you suspect unauthorized access

Tip: Regularly review your active sessions, especially after using public computers.
