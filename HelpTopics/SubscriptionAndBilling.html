Payment Methods

Accepted Payment Methods

How do I add a payment method?
  1. Navigate to Settings > Subscription & Billing
  2. Click on "Payment Methods"
  3. Select "Add New Payment Method"
  4. Enter your card details or select PayPal
  5. Click "Save" to add the payment method
How do I update my payment information?

To update your existing payment method:

  1. Go to Settings > Subscription & Billing > Payment Methods
  2. Find the payment method you want to update
  3. Click the "Edit" button
  4. Update the necessary information
  5. Click "Save Changes"
Is my payment information secure?

Yes, absolutely! We take payment security very seriously:

  • All payment data is encrypted using industry-standard SSL/TLS
  • We use PCI-DSS compliant payment processors
  • Card details are never stored on our servers
  • Two-factor authentication available for added security

Billing History & Invoices

Access Your Invoices

You can view and download all your past invoices from your account settings.

  1. Go to Settings > Subscription & Billing
  2. Click on "Billing History"
  3. View all your past transactions
  4. Click "Download PDF" next to any invoice to save it
When will I be charged?

Billing cycles depend on your subscription type:

  • Monthly: You'll be charged on the same day each month
  • Annual: You'll be charged once per year on your subscription anniversary
  • You'll receive an email notification 3 days before each charge
  • Failed payments will be retried automatically for 7 days
What if a payment fails?

If a payment fails, here's what happens:

  1. You'll receive an email notification immediately
  2. We'll automatically retry the payment after 3 days
  3. If it fails again, we'll retry after 7 days
  4. After 3 failed attempts, your subscription will be downgraded to Free
  5. You can update your payment method anytime to reactivate
Can I get a refund?

We offer refunds under certain conditions:

  • Full refund within 7 days of initial subscription
  • Partial refunds considered for technical issues
  • No refunds for partial months or annual subscriptions after 30 days
  • Contact support with your invoice number to request a refund

Refunds typically process within 5-10 business days.
